Compliance & Responsible Sourcing

Documented for high-risk origins.

Where an origin is classified by the EU as a conflict-affected and high-risk area (CAHRA), we apply the enhanced due diligence that classification requires — in line with EU Regulation 2017/821 and the OECD Guidance — and document it shipment by shipment.

CAHRA Due Diligence

Origin risk assessment before any commitment. Independent geological study and mine-visit reports. Traceability records for every shipment.

KYC & Sanctions Transparency

Corporate registration and beneficial ownership disclosed on request. Screening against U.S., EU, UK, and UN sanctions lists. Buyer KYC formats completed as standard.

Independent Verification

Sampling, assay, and mine inspection by independent third parties. Analysis certificates govern settlement.

The shipment file

Every cargo travels with a complete documentary pack:

  • Valid mining licence
  • Business licence & registration
  • Certificate of origin
  • Geological study & mine-visit reports
  • Production traceability records
  • Export declaration (customs)
  • Inland transport & container records
  • Warehouse in/out receipts
  • Stuffing report
  • Independent analysis certificate
  • Tax & royalty payment records
  • Packing list & bill of lading

We do not trade material of unverified origin.
